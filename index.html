☛ Babar Azam was selected to play for the Pakistani cricket team in the year 2015. He made his debut in the T20I format of the game against Zimbabwe in Lahore, Pakistan, on May 31, 2015. Later that year, in October, he made his ODI debut against Zimbabwe in Harare. Babar also made his Test debut for Pakistan against the West Indies in Dubai in October 2016.

☛ Babar Azam scored his first ODI century against the West Indies in Sharjah, United Arab Emirates, on October 5, 2016. He scored 120 runs off 131 balls in that match, which helped Pakistan win the game by 111 runs. With this century, Babar became the third Pakistani batsman to score a hundred in each of his first three ODI series, joining Zaheer Abbas and Saeed Anwar in achieving this feat.
